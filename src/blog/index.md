# Welcome to My Blog 👋

Hey, I'm Robin. I'm a software engineer who spends most of my time building things that probably shouldn't work-but somehow do. This blog is where I share what I've learned (and what I'm still figuring out) about software development.

Here's what I usually write about:

* **Game development** - engine architecture, gameplay mechanics, rendering, and other behind-the-scenes stuff.
* **Web development** - modern tools, clean practices, patterns, and lessons from real projects.
* **Software engineering in general** - tutorials, experiments, and thoughts on coding as a craft and a career.
* **Guides, snippets, and deep dives** - quick tips or more detailed explorations.

Lately, I've been working on **building a custom game engine in C++**, and I'll be sharing code, diagrams, and the occasional horror debug session.

This blog also pairs with my YouTube videos: if you like reading, you can get the theory here; if you like watching, you can see it in action over [there](https://www.youtube.com/@mrrobinofficial).

I'll also talk a bit about my own journey. The struggles, the wins, and the messy reality of life as a developer. Not everything is clean commits and perfect builds, and that's okay.

Stick around, and I hope you'll pick up something useful, discover a new idea, or at least feel a bit less alone in your coding challenges. Software development is hard, but it's also rewarding as hell once you get the hang of it!
